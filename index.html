🎯 Your 99 Percentile Roadmap — JEE 2027

6-Phase precision curriculum. Built by 3 experts. Designed to take you from scratch to AIR < 1000 in exactly 6 months.

Phase 0
⚡ Foundation Sprint
Jul 23 – Aug 3 • 2 Weeks
BEFORE touching any chapter — master these prerequisites. This is what separates AIR 1000 from AIR 10000.
  • 📐 Math: All trig identities, quadratic equations, sets & functions
  • 🧪 Chem: Mole Concept complete (n=m/M, molarity, stoichiometry)
  • ⚡ Physics: Vectors, scalars, unit vectors, dot & cross product
  • 📐 Math: Straight Lines + Matrices & Determinants + Complex Numbers
  • 🧪 Chem: Chemical Bonding (hybridization, VSEPR, MOT)
  • ⚡ Physics: Kinematics + Newton's Laws + FBD mastery
🎯 Goal: Prerequisites cleared. Ready to start real JEE prep.
Phase 1
🏗️ Core Build — Month 1
Aug 4 – Aug 31 • 4 Weeks
Foundation mechanics + organic chemistry base + algebra. These chapters unlock everything that comes after.
  • 📐 Math: Vectors, 3D Geometry, Permutations & Combinations, Binomial Theorem
  • ⚡ Physics: Work-Energy-Power, Rotational Motion, Gravitation
  • 🧪 Chem: GOC (complete), Hydrocarbons (Alkanes, Alkenes, Alkynes, Benzene)
🎯 Goal: Mock test — aim 20–35 percentile. Foundation is in place.
Phase 2
📈 Power Build — Month 2
Sep 1 – Sep 30 • 4 Weeks
The high-stakes electricity + equilibrium + coordinate geometry trio. Most marks concentration in this phase.
  • 📐 Math: Circles, Conic Sections (Parabola, Ellipse, Hyperbola), Limits & Continuity
  • ⚡ Physics: Electrostatics + Capacitance, Current Electricity (complete)
  • 🧪 Chem: Chemical Thermodynamics, Chemical Equilibrium (Chem + Ionic)
🎯 Goal: Mock test — 50–65 percentile. Electrostatics + Equilibrium scoring.
Phase 3
🚀 Calculus + Reactions — Month 3
Oct 1 – Oct 31 • 4 Weeks
Calculus is 35% of JEE Math. Organic reactions = Chemistry goldmine. Both mastered this month.
  • 📐 Math: Derivatives, Application of Derivatives (AoD), Probability
  • ⚡ Physics: Optics (Ray + Wave complete), Thermodynamics + Kinetic Theory
  • 🧪 Chem: Electrochemistry, Chemical Kinetics, Aldehydes & Ketones
🎯 Goal: Mock test — 70–80 percentile. Calculus + Optics dominating.
Phase 4
🔥 Integration + Inorganic — Month 4
Nov 1 – Nov 30 • 4 Weeks
Integration is the KING chapter. Spend the WHOLE month here. Plus Modern Physics + Coordination Compounds.
  • 📐 Math: Integration (full month — indefinite + definite + properties), Differential Equations, Area
  • ⚡ Physics: Modern Physics (complete), EMI + AC Circuits
  • 🧪 Chem: Coordination Compounds, P-Block Elements, Amines + Biomolecules
🎯 Goal: Mock test — 85–90 percentile. Integration + Modern Physics = big score jump.
Phase 5
🎯 PYQ Mode — Month 5
Dec 1 – Dec 31 • 4 Weeks
Zero new chapters after Dec 15. Full PYQ mode. Last 5 years JEE Mains solved chapter-wise. Speed + accuracy.
  • 📐 Math: Magnetism + remaining topics + PYQ solving all chapters
  • ⚡ Physics: Waves, SHM, Magnetism + PYQ all chapters
  • 🧪 Chem: D&F Block, Solutions + full NCERT Inorganic revision + PYQ
  • ⚠️ No new topics after Dec 15!
🎯 Goal: Mock test — 92–96 percentile consistently.
Phase 6
🏆 Mock Marathon — Final Push
Jan 1 – Jan 24 • 3 Weeks
3 full mocks per week. Error analysis after every mock. Formula revision every morning. Zero new content.
  • Full mock (3 hrs) — Mon, Wed, Fri
  • Error analysis (2 hrs) — after every mock
  • Formula sheet revision — 30 min every morning
  • Error notebook review — every Sunday
  • 🎯 Jan 24, 2027 — JEE Mains Session 1
🎯 Goal: 99+ Percentile. One shot. No regrets. 🔥
